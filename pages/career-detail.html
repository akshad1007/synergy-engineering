Application Engineer

location_on Thane, MH
work Full-time
bolt Engineering Dept.

Job Description

At Synergy Engineering, we are looking for a highly skilled Application Engineer to bridge the gap between our complex industrial products and our clients' specific engineering requirements. You will be the technical pivot point, ensuring that our solutions are precisely machined to meet 0.01mm tolerances in real-world applications.

You will work closely with the R&D and Sales teams to provide architectural-level technical support, develop specialized schematics, and oversee the implementation of precision engineering components across various heavy-duty industrial sectors.

Key Responsibilities

  • check_circle Translate customer requirements into detailed technical specifications and architectural engineering designs.
  • check_circle Conduct on-site technical evaluations and performance audits for industrial equipment in Thane and regional hubs.
  • check_circle Collaborate with the manufacturing team to ensure 100% design-to-production accuracy.
  • check_circle Provide technical training to clients and internal sales teams on high-precision component integration.

Requirements

Education

B.Tech/M.Tech in Mechanical or Industrial Engineering.

Experience

4-6 years in B2B industrial engineering environments.

Technical Skills

Expertise in AutoCAD, SolidWorks, and Precision Measurement systems.

Communication

Fluent English and Marathi for effective stakeholder management.
