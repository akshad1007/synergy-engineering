Brother PT-E850TKW

Industrial Tube & Label Printer

Product Overview

The Brother PT-E850TKW is a professional industrial tube and label printer designed for electrical, telecommunications, and industrial applications. Its unique dual engine design allows printing both heat shrink tubes and laminated labels without changing supplies.

Key Features

  • Dual Engine Design: Print tubes and labels without changing supplies
  • 360 dpi Resolution: Crisp, clear professional output
  • Fast Printing: Up to 60 mm/sec for labels, 40 mm/sec for tubes
  • Multiple Connectivity: USB, LAN, Wi-Fi, mobile printing
  • Standalone Operation: Built-in QWERTY keyboard and LCD
  • Auto Cutter: Clean and precise label cuts
  • Durable Labels: Weatherproof, waterproof TZe tapes

Specifications

Print Resolution 360 dpi (720 dpi high-res mode)
Print Speed Up to 60 mm/sec (labels)
Label Width 3.5mm to 36mm (TZe tapes)
Tube Support HSe 5.8-23.6mm, PVC 2.5-6.5mm
Connectivity USB 2.0, LAN, Wi-Fi
Memory 99 files, 40,000 characters
Weight 4.6 kg

Applications

  • Cable and wire identification
  • Panel and equipment labeling
  • Heat shrink tube marking
  • Electrical installation marking
  • Telecom infrastructure labeling
