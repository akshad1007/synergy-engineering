Brother PT-E110VP

Handheld Industrial Label Printer

Product Overview

The Brother PT-E110VP is a compact, handheld industrial label printer designed for electricians and datacom professionals. It creates durable laminated labels for cable marking, patch panels, and equipment identification with easy one-touch keys.

Key Features

  • Compact Design: Lightweight and portable
  • One-Touch Keys: Quick access to common label types
  • LCD Display: Easy label preview
  • Durable Labels: Laminated TZe tapes
  • Auto Cutter: Clean label cuts
  • Carrying Case: Included for portability

Specifications

Print Resolution 180 dpi
Print Speed Up to 20 mm/sec
Tape Width 3.5mm to 12mm
Display LCD (15 characters)
Power 6x AAA batteries or AC adapter
Weight ~400g

Applications

  • Cable and wire marking
  • Electrical panel labeling
  • Asset identification
  • Patch panel marking
