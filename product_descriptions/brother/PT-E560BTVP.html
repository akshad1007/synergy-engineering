Brother PT-E560BTVP

Industrial Wireless Label Printer

Product Overview

The Brother PT-E560BTVP is a professional handheld industrial label printer designed for electricians and datacom professionals. It features wireless Bluetooth connectivity, a color display, and the ability to print on both laminated labels and heat shrink tubes.

Key Features

  • Bluetooth Connectivity: Wireless printing from mobile devices
  • Color LCD: Easy-to-read backlit display
  • Dual Printing: Labels and heat shrink tubes
  • 180 dpi Resolution: Clear, professional labels
  • Fast Print Speed: Up to 30mm/sec
  • QWERTY Keyboard: Standalone operation
  • Durable Labels: Laminated TZe tapes

Specifications

Print Resolution 180 dpi
Print Speed Up to 30 mm/sec
Tape Width 3.5mm to 24mm
Connectivity USB, Bluetooth
Display Color LCD backlit
Power Li-ion battery or AC adapter
Weight ~640g (without battery)

Applications

  • Electrical panel labeling
  • Cable and wire identification
  • Patch panel marking
  • Facility management
  • Heat shrink tube printing
