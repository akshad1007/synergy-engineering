MTE dV/dt Filter

Motor Protection Filter for VFDs

Product Overview

The MTE dV/dt Filter is designed to protect motors from damaging voltage spikes caused by Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs). It limits both the peak voltage and rate of voltage rise (dV/dt) to safe levels, extending motor life and enabling longer cable runs.

Key Features

  • Voltage Spike Protection: Limits peak voltage to ≤1000V
  • dV/dt Limiting: Reduces rate of rise to ≤200 V/µs
  • Extended Cable Runs: Up to 1000 feet at 8 kHz
  • Motor Protection: Extends motor insulation life
  • Wide Current Range: 3A to 1200A ratings
  • Multiple Voltages: 208V to 600V systems

Specifications

Peak Voltage ≤1000V
dV/dt Output ≤200 V/µs
Voltage Class 208V, 230V, 460V, 575V, 600V
Current Range 3A to 1200A
Frequency 50/60 Hz
Switching Freq 2-16 kHz

Applications

  • Long motor cable runs
  • Motor retrofit applications
  • Inverter duty motor protection
  • General VFD installations
