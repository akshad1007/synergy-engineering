MTE Line Reactor

RL Series Input Line Reactor

Product Overview

MTE RL Series Line Reactors are designed to absorb power line disturbances, reduce nuisance tripping, lower harmonic distortion, and minimize the effects of long motor leads. They serve as robust filtering solutions for VFDs and DC drives.

Key Features

  • Surge Protection: 3% impedance provides 90% transient protection
  • Enhanced Protection: 5% impedance provides 99% transient protection
  • Harmonic Reduction: Lowers total harmonic distortion
  • Wide Current Range: 1A to 1,500A ratings
  • High Overload: 150% continuous for reactors <750A
  • Certifications: UL-508, CE, CSA C22.2

Specifications

Voltage Class 600V / 690V AC
Impedance 1.5%, 2%, 3%, 4%, 5%
Current Range 1A to 1,500A
Frequency 50/60 Hz
Operating Temp -40°C to +50°C
Insulation Class Class N (200°C)

Applications

  • VFD input protection
  • Harmonic mitigation
  • Nuisance trip prevention
  • Power conditioning
  • Industrial motor drives
