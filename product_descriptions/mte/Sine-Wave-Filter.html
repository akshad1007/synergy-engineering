MTE Sine Wave Filter

SineWave Guardian® Filter

Product Overview

The MTE SineWave Guardian® Filter converts the PWM output of Variable Frequency Drives into a near-perfect sine wave. This eliminates high-frequency content and voltage peaks, protecting motors and cables while reducing heating, noise, and EMI.

Key Features

  • PWM to Sine Wave: Clean sinusoidal output for motor protection
  • Long Cable Runs: Enables up to 15,000 feet cable distance
  • dV/dt Elimination: Removes harmful voltage spikes
  • Noise Reduction: Significantly reduces motor audible noise
  • EMI Reduction: Minimizes electromagnetic interference
  • Motor Protection: Extends motor insulation life

Specifications

Voltage Ranges 200-230V, 380-480V, 550-600V
Motor Sizes 1.5 HP to 700 HP
THV Output <5% at full load
Switching Freq 2 kHz to 8 kHz
Current Rating 100% RMS continuous
Enclosures Panel, NEMA 1, 2, 3R

Applications

  • Submersible pump systems
  • Long cable run installations
  • Oil field equipment
  • Wind turbines
  • Steel mills
  • HVAC systems
