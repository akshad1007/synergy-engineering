MTE Harmonic Filter

Matrix® AP Advanced Passive Harmonic Filter

Product Overview

The MTE Matrix® AP Harmonic Filter is an advanced passive filter that adapts to varying power loads, ensuring superior harmonic mitigation for VFD applications. Guaranteed to meet IEEE-519 standards with less than 5% Total Harmonic Current Distortion (THID) at full load.

Key Features

  • IEEE-519 Compliant: Less than 5% THID at 100% load
  • Adaptive Technology: Automatically adjusts to varying loads
  • Low kVAR: Generator compatible design
  • Wide Voltage Range: Up to 690V systems
  • High Current Capacity: Up to 1600A
  • Multiple Enclosures: Open panel, NEMA 1, 2, or 3R

Specifications

THID at 100% Load <5%
THID at 30% Load <8%
Voltage Range Up to 690V
Current Range Up to 1600A
Frequency 50/60 Hz
Compliance IEEE-519

Applications

  • Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs)
  • Oil and gas installations
  • Water/wastewater treatment
  • HVAC systems
  • Pumps, fans, and compressors
