MTE RFI Filter

EMC/RFI Filter for VFD Applications

Product Overview

MTE RFI Filters are designed to reduce Radio Frequency Interference (RFI) and Electromagnetic Interference (EMI) generated by Variable Frequency Drives. They ensure compliance with CE and EMC standards while protecting sensitive equipment from interference.

Key Features

  • EMC Compliant: Meets CE directive requirements
  • High Frequency Filtering: 150 kHz to 30 MHz range
  • Low Insertion Loss: Minimal voltage drop
  • Wide Current Range: 3A to 1000A ratings
  • Multiple Voltages: 230V to 600V systems
  • Compact Design: Easy installation

Specifications

Frequency Range 150 kHz to 30 MHz
Voltage Class 230V, 460V, 575V, 600V
Current Range 3A to 1000A
Categories C1, C2, C3
Operating Temp -25°C to +45°C
Compliance CE, EMC Directive

Applications

  • VFD input filtering
  • EMC compliance solutions
  • Sensitive equipment protection
  • Industrial environments
