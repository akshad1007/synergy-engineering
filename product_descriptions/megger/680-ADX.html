Megger ADX Series

Automated Static Motor Analyzer

Product Overview

The Megger ADX Series is a sophisticated automated static motor analyzer designed for comprehensive insulation and winding testing of electric motors, generators, and coils. It offers a wide range of features and specifications tailored for assessing motor health, monitoring trends, and locating faults in various industrial environments.

Key Features

  • Large 10.4" Industrial Touchscreen: Daylight-viewable color display for clear visibility indoors and outdoors
  • High Voltage Testing: Available in models up to 4 kV, 6 kV, 12 kV, or 15 kV (extendable to 40 kV with optional Power Pack)
  • EAR+â„¢ Technology: Advanced surge analysis with partial discharge (PD) measurement for early insulation weakness detection
  • Cloud Connectivity: Auto-sync with PowerDB Dashboard for advanced analysis, reporting, and archiving
  • Rugged IP54-rated Housing: Built for demanding industrial environments
  • Android-based System: Fast performance with modern user experience

Testing Capabilities

High Voltage Tests
  • Insulation Resistance (IR)
  • Dielectric Absorption (DA)
  • Polarization Index (PI)
  • DC HiPot (Standard, Ramped, Step)
  • Surge Analysis with PD
Low Voltage Tests (RLC Option)
  • Winding Resistance
  • Inductance
  • Capacitance
  • Impedance & Phase Angle
  • Bar-to-bar Testing (DC Motors)

Specifications

Test Voltage Up to 4 kV / 6 kV / 12 kV / 15 kV (model dependent)
Extended Voltage Up to 30 kV or 40 kV with PPX Power Pack
Display 10.4" Industrial-grade Color Touchscreen
Input Power 90 - 264 V, 47-63 Hz, 2.5 A, CAT II 300 V
Protection Rating IP54
Connectivity Wi-Fi, Ethernet, USB, HDMI
Safety IEC61010-compliant, Automatic Grounding

Applications

  • Electric motor maintenance and diagnostics
  • Generator testing and condition assessment
  • Coil and winding analysis
  • Predictive and preventive maintenance programs
  • Quality control in manufacturing
