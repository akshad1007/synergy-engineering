Megger MIT5252 Series

Advanced 5kV/10kV/15kV Insulation Resistance Tester

Product Overview

The Megger MIT5252 Series (MIT5252, MIT10252, MIT15252) are advanced diagnostic insulation testers offering 5kV, 10kV, and 15kV test voltages. They provide comprehensive insulation analysis including PI, DAR, DD, SV, and ramp testing with large internal memory.

Key Features

  • High Voltage: Up to 15kV output (model dependent)
  • Ultra High Range: Up to 35 TΩ measurement
  • Advanced Diagnostics: PI, DAR, DD, SV, Ramp tests
  • Large Memory: 1000 test results storage
  • Color Display: Graphical LCD with real-time trending
  • Temperature Correction: Automatic TC40 and TC20
  • Bluetooth/USB: Data transfer connectivity

Specifications

Test Voltages 250V to 5kV/10kV/15kV
IR Range 10 kΩ to 35 TΩ
Diagnostic Tests PI, DAR, DD, SV, Ramp
Memory 1000 results
Connectivity USB, Bluetooth
Safety CAT IV 600V

Applications

  • Transformer insulation testing
  • High voltage cable testing
  • Generator winding analysis
  • Motor insulation diagnostics
