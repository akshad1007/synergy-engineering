Megger DCM340

AC/DC Clamp Meter

Product Overview

The Megger DCM340 is a professional AC/DC clamp meter designed for electrical testing and maintenance. It features true RMS measurement, large jaw opening, and rugged construction for reliable current measurement in demanding environments.

Key Features

  • True RMS: Accurate AC and DC current measurement
  • Wide Range: Up to 600A AC/DC
  • Large Jaw: 34mm diameter for thick cables
  • Data Hold: Freeze readings for easy recording
  • Auto Power Off: Battery saving feature
  • CAT III 600V: High safety rating

Specifications

AC Current Range 0 to 600A TRMS
DC Current Range 0 to 600A
Jaw Opening 34mm diameter
Resolution 0.1A
Safety Rating CAT III 600V
Display 4000 count LCD

Applications

  • Industrial current measurement
  • Electrical maintenance
  • Panel board testing
  • Motor current checks
