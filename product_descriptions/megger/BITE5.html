Megger BITE5

Battery Impedance Tester

Product Overview

The Megger BITE5 is a versatile battery impedance tester designed for assessing the condition of lead-acid (VLA and VRLA), NiCD, and lithium-ion batteries. It measures and records key parameters including battery voltage, impedance, and temperature. The device also supports comprehensive discharge testing for complete battery health assessment.

Key Features

  • Multi-Battery Support: Test lead-acid (VLA/VRLA), NiCD, and lithium-ion batteries
  • High Precision: Impedance measurement with 0.1 µΩ resolution
  • Wide Voltage Range: DC voltage up to 1000V, AC voltage up to 600V
  • Touchscreen Interface: Easy setup and trending display
  • Wireless Connectivity: Transfer data to PowerDB software for analysis
  • IP54 Protected: Rugged design for field use
  • Long Battery Life: Over 8 hours operation on internal battery

Measurements

  • Cell Impedance
  • Cell Voltage
  • Cell Temperature
  • Voltage Ripple
  • AC/DC Current
  • Discharge Testing

Specifications

DC Voltage Range Up to 1000 V
