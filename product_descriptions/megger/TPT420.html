Megger TPT420

Two-Pole Voltage & Continuity Tester

Product Overview

The Megger TPT420 is a professional two-pole voltage and continuity tester compliant with IEC 61243-3 (GS38). It provides safe, reliable voltage presence detection with LED and LCD indication, built-in flashlight, and phase rotation testing.

Key Features

  • Wide Voltage Range: 12V to 690V AC/DC
  • CAT IV 600V: Highest safety category
  • LED Indicators: Quick voltage level indication
  • LCD Display: Precise voltage reading
  • Continuity Test: With buzzer indication
  • Phase Rotation: Tests 3-phase sequence
  • Built-in Torch: Work light for dark areas

Specifications

Voltage Range 12V to 690V AC/DC
Continuity Range 0 to 500 kΩ
Safety Rating CAT IV 600V, CAT III 690V
Display LCD + LED bar
GS38 Compliant Yes
Protection IP64

Applications

  • Voltage presence detection
  • Safe isolation verification
  • Continuity testing
  • Phase rotation check
