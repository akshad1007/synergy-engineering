Megger BM5200

5 kV Insulation Resistance Tester

Product Overview

The Megger BM5200 is a 5kV insulation resistance tester designed for high-voltage insulation resistance testing in industrial applications. Perfect for testing cables, rotating machinery, transformers, and switchgear with measurement range up to 1 TΩ.

Key Features

  • Five Test Voltages: 250V, 500V, 1000V, 2500V, and 5000V DC
  • High Resistance Range: 100 kΩ to 1 TΩ (1000 GΩ)
  • Multiple Test Modes: Insulation (InS), Polarization Index (PI), and variable timed test
  • Dual Display: Digital readout with analog arc display
  • Guard Terminal: Minimizes surface leakage effects
  • Battery Powered: ~5 hours operation at 5kV into 100MΩ
  • CAT III 600V Safety: Industrial grade protection

Test Modes

InS Mode

Standard insulation resistance test

PI Mode

Polarization Index for insulation quality

Timed Test

Variable duration testing

Specifications

Test Voltages 250V, 500V, 1000V, 2500V, 5000V DC

Power 8 x AA Batteries
Battery Life ~5 hours continuous at 5kV
Protection IP40

Applications

  • Cable insulation testing
  • Motor and generator testing
  • Transformer insulation assessment
  • Switchgear maintenance
  • Preventive maintenance programs
