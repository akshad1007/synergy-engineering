Megger DELTA4000 Series

12 kV Power Factor/Tan Delta Tester

Product Overview

The Megger DELTA4000 Series is an automatic insulation power factor/dissipation factor (tan delta) test set for assessing electrical insulation condition in transformers, bushings, circuit breakers, cables, and rotating machinery with industry-leading noise suppression.

Key Features

  • High Noise Suppression: Up to 15mA interference handling
  • 12 kV Output: Continuously adjustable 0-12 kV
  • 1 Hz Power Factor: Enhanced low-frequency testing
  • Patented ITC: Individual Temperature Correction
  • Portable Design: Rugged two-piece for field use
  • PowerDB Integration: Auto testing and reporting

Specifications

Output Voltage 0 to 12 kV
Test Frequency 1-505 Hz
Output Power 3.6 kVA
Capacitance Range 0 to 100 µF
PF Resolution 0.001%
Operating Temp -20 to +55°C

Applications

  • Power transformer testing
  • Bushing insulation assessment
  • Circuit breaker testing
  • Cable insulation analysis
