Megger MIT410/2 Series

1kV Insulation Resistance Tester

Product Overview

The Megger MIT410/2 is a compact, rugged 1kV insulation resistance tester designed for industrial and commercial electrical testing. It combines insulation testing with continuity, resistance, and voltage measurement in a single handheld instrument.

Key Features

  • Four Test Voltages: 250V, 500V, and 1000V DC
  • High IR Range: Up to 200 GΩ
  • Hybrid Display: Digital with analog arc for trending
  • PI and DAR: Automatic Polarization Index and Dielectric Absorption Ratio
  • Pass/Fail Limits: Programmable limit comparators
  • CAT IV 600V: Highest safety rating
  • Rugged Design: IP54 rated, drop tested

Specifications

Test Voltages 250V, 500V, 1000V DC
IR Range 10 kΩ to 200 GΩ
Continuity Range 0.01 Ω to 1 MΩ
AC Voltage 30V to 600V TRMS
Safety Rating CAT IV 600V
Protection IP54
Memory 99 results

Applications

  • Cable insulation testing
  • Motor winding resistance
  • Electrical installation verification
  • Industrial maintenance
